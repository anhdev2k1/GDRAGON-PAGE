Profile

EARLY MUSICAL CAREER

Born and raised in Seoul, South Korea, Kwon Jiyong started his career at the age of 5 when he joined the group Little Roora. After the group released their Christmas album, their agency asked for a sudden termination of the contract, which shocked Jiyong and swore to his mother that he would "never become a singer" again. In 3rd grade, Jiyong happened to know the American group Wu-Tang Clan, and since then he started to learn to rapping. Jiyong also wrote music when he was very young. Jiyong's first widely publicized self-composed song "Love Letter" was written when he entered the school's talent contest. While still looking for a leader, he met hip-hop group People's Crew. Under the group's guidance, he participated in the hip-hop album "2001 Korea HipHopFlex" in 2001. G-Dragon appeared in public as a new member of the hip-hop group People's Crew. and group composer. Although he wrote the lyrics for his own song, he admitted that "I was young, so my English was very bad at the time so the lyrics content was nothing new, it was just about 'I'm young, but I'm Best'".

Along with Big Bang, G-Dragon also has some solo activities. After assisting Taeyang in producing his debut album Hot , he also recor...ded his own version of the album's title track "Only Look At Me" (Korean: 나만 바라 봐; Romaja: Naman Barabwa), and name it "Only Look At Me Part 2" . This version is only released online. Along with Taeyang and T.O.P, G-Dragon was also featured in the song "Super Fly" in the album Rush of singer Lexy. In May 2009, he collaborated with Japanese group W-inds on their single titled "Rain Is Fallin '/ Hybrid Dream".

On October 5, 2011, G-Dragon was notified of a positive cannabis test result. Although his initial urine test was negative, when he tested his hair sample, the results were completely opposite. G-Dragon finally confessed that he was taking the drug at a nightclub in Japan while attending a concert. G-Dragon said: “I smoked a cigarette that someone gave me in the club. Its smell was different from a normal cigarette, so I was a little suspicious that it was cannabis, but the I do smoke. " The scandal caused the promotions with Big Bang to be halted, and YG Entertainment announced that they would give him time to rethink his mistake. advertising contracts and then reappearing in public after the scandal at MTV EMA 2011 with Big Bang.
